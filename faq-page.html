Pipeline & Follow-Up

It's a one-time audit of your contact database. I go through everything, every lead, every past client, and organize them by status, priority, and readiness. I set up your pipeline board, create status labels, build automation templates, and separate DNC contacts. By the end, you have a clean, actionable system instead of a mess.

A minimum of 50 outbound calls per week. That covers your full pipeline: distressed leads, active buyers and sellers, past clients, and referral contacts. I follow a proven 4×3 cadence: 3 calls, 3 texts, 3 more calls, then a drip campaign. Each contact gets multiple touchpoints before being moved to nurture.

I hand them to you immediately with full context. You get a text with the lead's name, address, and what they said, followed by a phone briefing. If you're unavailable, I give the lead a specific callback window and follow up within 24 hours. No warm lead goes cold on my watch.

Every week we get on a quick call or video chat to review your pipeline health. We go over call volume, leads advanced, handoffs made, and strategy adjustments. It keeps us both accountable and makes sure your pipeline stays on track toward your goals. Not just alive, but actually growing.
