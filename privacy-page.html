Privacy Policy

Your information,
handled with care.

Here's exactly what we collect, how we use it, and how to opt out of communications at any time.

Effective Date: July 11, 2026

1. Who We Are

This Privacy Policy covers MomoPromo LLC ("MomoPromo," "we," "us"), a Phoenix, Arizona company operated by Morgan Kellar. Morgan Kellar is also a licensed Arizona Mortgage Loan Originator (NMLS #1708385) at Equity Smart Home Loans (NMLS #856170). This policy applies to momopromo.ai, www.momopromo.ai, and any forms, booking pages, webinar registrations, and chat tools connected to them.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you choose to give us when you:

  • Fill out a contact or lead form on this site or our landing pages
  • Register for a webinar or workshop (such as the Phoenix First-Time Homebuyer Webinar)
  • Book a call through our scheduling widget
  • Message us through the chat widget on this site
  • Email, call, or text us directly

That information typically includes your name, email address, phone number, and anything you tell us about your goals, such as your homebuying timeline or your business needs. We also collect standard technical data automatically, like browser type, device, pages visited, and approximate location, through cookies and similar technologies.

What we do not collect here: this website does not ask for Social Security numbers, bank account numbers, credit card numbers, or full loan application details. If you move forward with a mortgage pre-approval or loan, that application happens through Equity Smart Home Loans' secure systems and is governed by their privacy notices and federal financial privacy law (the Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act).

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your questions and requests
  • To schedule and confirm calls, consultations, and webinar attendance
  • To send you the information you asked for, such as webinar links, replays, and homebuyer education resources
  • To follow up about mortgage services or marketing services you expressed interest in
  • To send occasional marketing emails and texts you can opt out of at any time
  • To improve this website and our services

We do not sell your personal information. Ever.

4. Calls and Text Messages (TCPA Consent)

Your Consent, In Plain English

By submitting a form, registering for a webinar, or booking a call on this site, you agree that MomoPromo LLC and Morgan Kellar may contact you by phone call, text message (SMS), and email at the contact information you provided, including through automated technology, about the services you inquired about. Consent is not a condition of purchasing any property, goods, or services. Message and data rates may apply, and message frequency varies.

How to opt out

  • Text messages: reply STOP to any text to stop receiving messages. Reply HELP for help.
  • Phone calls: tell us on any call that you'd like to stop receiving calls, or email us at momo.kellar@gmail.com with "Do Not Call" in the subject line.
  • Everything at once: email us and ask to be removed from all outreach. We'll honor it promptly.

We maintain an internal do-not-call list and honor the National Do Not Call Registry. Opt-out requests are recorded and respected across our systems.

5. Email Communications (CAN-SPAM)

Every marketing email we send includes a working unsubscribe link. Clicking it stops marketing emails from us, and we honor unsubscribe requests within 10 business days as required by the CAN-SPAM Act, though it's usually immediate. We may still send you transactional messages you've requested, such as a booking confirmation or a webinar link.

6. How Your Information Is Stored and Shared

Contact information submitted through this site flows into our customer relationship management (CRM) platform, provided by HighLevel Inc. (also known as GoHighLevel or LeadConnector). That's how we keep track of conversations, send confirmations, and make sure your opt-out choices are honored everywhere. HighLevel processes this data on our behalf under its own privacy and security commitments.

We share personal information only with:

  • Service providers that help us run the business, such as our CRM, scheduling, and email tools
  • Equity Smart Home Loans, if you ask to be connected for mortgage pre-approval or a loan
  • Your real estate agent, only when you've asked us to coordinate with them
  • Authorities, if required by law

7. Your Rights and Choices

You can ask us at any time to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you, or to stop contacting you. Email momo.kellar@gmail.com and we'll take care of it. If you're a resident of a state with a consumer privacy law that grants additional rights, we'll honor those rights too.

8. Cookies and Analytics

This site uses cookies and similar technologies for basic functionality and to understand how visitors use the site. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling them may affect features like the chat widget.

9. Children

This site is not directed to anyone under 18, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has submitted information to us, contact us and we'll delete it.

10. Data Retention and Security

We keep contact records as long as needed to serve you and meet legal and licensing obligations, then delete or anonymize them. We use commercially reasonable safeguards to protect your information, though no online system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

11. Changes to This Policy

If we make meaningful changes, we'll update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after changes means you accept the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

Questions about this policy or your information? Reach out anytime.
