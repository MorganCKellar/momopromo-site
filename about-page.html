I didn't take the straight path. That's kind of the point.

My real estate roots run deep. I've worked as a Mortgage Loan Officer at AZ Home Loans and as a mortgage banker at Quicken Loans, where clients consistently praised me for being responsive, calm under pressure, and genuinely easy to work with. I studied at Arizona State University's W. P. Carey School of Business. I understand the back end of real estate transactions because I've lived inside them. My mortgage background gives me a unique edge: I understand both sides of the transaction. MomoPromo is how I bring that expertise to real estate agents who want a partner, not just another tool.

Before real estate, I spent years in live event production. I was hired as the Venue Manager and Primary Production Manager at Walter Productions, where I ran the kind of events that have a hundred moving parts and zero margin for error. Vendor coordination, timeline management, on-the-ground logistics, staffing, troubleshooting in real time. I've also helped produce events at the ghost town of Cleator, Arizona, a former mining settlement in the Bradshaw Mountains that's been transformed into a creative hub for entrepreneurs, artists, and adventurers. Producing events taught me something no classroom ever could: how to manage chaos, coordinate people, keep timelines tight, and deliver when everything is moving at once. That's the exact same skill set I bring to your pipeline.

Outside of real estate and events, I serve as a board member of the Bureau of Erotic Discourse (B.E.D.), a volunteer-run consent education nonprofit that works directly with Burning Man Emergency Services. We've built free educational curricula through BEDtalks.org, and I've presented at the official Burning Man Camp Symposium helping camps across the playa develop their own consent policies. The organization is 100% grassroots, 100% volunteer, and it taught me more about building systems, leading teams, and communicating with real humans than any corporate job ever could.

All of that might sound scattered. It's not. Every role I've ever held has one thing in common: I show up for people, and I follow through. That's exactly what your pipeline needs.
